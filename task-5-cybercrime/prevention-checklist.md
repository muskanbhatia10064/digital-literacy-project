# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist for College Students

## General Online Safety
1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or password with anyone — 
   not even bank representatives
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all your accounts
3. Use strong, unique passwords for each platform you use
4. Always verify the identity of anyone contacting you 
   about money or personal details
5. Never click on suspicious links received via SMS, 
   WhatsApp, or email

## Financial & UPI Safety
6. Remember — receiving money NEVER requires entering your UPI PIN. 
   If someone asks you to enter your PIN to receive money, it is a SCAM
7. Never scan a QR code sent by a stranger — QR codes are 
   used to TAKE money, not send it
8. Regularly check your bank statements and UPI transaction 
   history for any suspicious activity

## If You Are a Victim
- Immediately call the National Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
- Report online at: cybercrime.gov.in
- Block your UPI and bank account immediately via your bank's 
  helpline
- Save all evidence — screenshots, messages, transaction IDs
